Title: Oil firm on OPEC cut hopes
Date: 20161122
Content:
Investing.com - Oil Tuesday remained firm after strong overnight gains on optimism of an OPEC deal to cut output.U.S. crude was up 2 cents, or 0.04%, at $48.26 a barrel at 07:15 ET.. Brent crude put on 0.08% to $48.94 a barrel.OPEC plans to cut output to 32.5-33 million barrels per day. It is aiming to formalize a deal at a meeting in Vienna on November 30.A Nigerian delegate to an OPEC meeting of experts in Vienna said he expects all members to come "on board."Oil could plummet in the absence of a deal but the planned cut will make scant inroads into the global supply glut.The dollar index was slightly higher. A stronger dollar weakens demand for oil.
