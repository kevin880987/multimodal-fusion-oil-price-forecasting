Title: API says crude stocks up 3.65 million barrels last week
Date: 20161115
Content:
Investing.com - The American Petroleum Institute (API) said Tuesday crude oil inventories rose 3.65 million barrels last week, building on an increase of 4.4 million barrels the previous week and rose more than expected. Gasoline inventories recorded a slight draw of 160,000 barrels in the week while there was an increase in distillate inventories of 3.0 million barrels, the first gain in six weeks. Cushing recorded a build of 1.13 million barrels after a slight increase the previous week.
