Title: Exxon selling stake in Canada's Terra Nova project
Date: 20180227
Content:
HOUSTON (Reuters) -  Exxon Mobil  Corp (N:XOM) is selling its 19-percent stake in the Terra Nova project off the eastern coast of Canada, according to data from Schlumberger's (N:SLB) oil and gas asset sale business.  An Exxon representative was not immediately available to comment.
