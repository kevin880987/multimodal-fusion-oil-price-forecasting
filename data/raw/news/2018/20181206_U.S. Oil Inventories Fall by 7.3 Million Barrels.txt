Title: U.S. Oil Inventories Fall by 7.3 Million Barrels
Date: 20181206
Content:
Investing.com - Oil inventories in the U.S. fell by 7.3 million barrels in the latest week, the Energy Information Administration said Thursday. The drawdown snapped a streak of builds and confounded expectations for drop of fewer than 1 million barrels. Imports for the week ended Nov. 30 fell by 1.7 million barrels. Crude oil prices were down 3.2%, paring losses after the numbers were released.
