Title: U.S. crude stocks down 1.661 mn barrels vs. forecast fall of 2.739 mn
Date: 20170614
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks fell less than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories fell by 1.661 mn barrels after a rise of 3.259 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 2.739 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks rose by 2.096 mn barrels after a rise of 3.324 mn barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to fall by 457,000 barrels.WTI extended losses after the data and was off 1.29% at $45.86.
