Title: API says crude stocks fell 1.28 million barrels last week, gasoline up
Date: 20161122
Content:
Investing.com - The American Petroleum Institute said Tuesday that crude oil inventories fell 1.28 million barrels at the end of last week, following a build of 3.65 million barrels the previous week and breaking three straight weekly builds. Gasoline inventories jumped 2.7 million barrels last week and distillates fell by 350,000 barrels, marking six declines in the last seven weeks. Cushing saw a draw of 140,000 barrels, following a rise of 1.13 million barrels the previous week.
