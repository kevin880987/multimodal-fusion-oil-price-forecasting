Title: U.S. crude stocks fall 1.882 mn barrels vs. forecast rise of 2.995 mn
Date: 20160928
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks unexpectedly fell in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude stocks fell by 1.882 mn barrels after a fall of 6.2 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to rise by 2.995 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks rose by 2.072 mn barrels after a fall of 3.2 mn barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were forecast to rise by 178,000 barrels.U.S. crude was up 44 cents, or 0.99%, at $45.11 after data release.
