Title: Oil prices rise as traders close short Brent positions
Date: 20160226
Content:
Investing.com - Crude oil prices flipped on Friday, reversing falls in the morning as traders closed short positions Rises wre driven by short positions being closed ahead of Brent's expiry next week US crude was supported by strong gasoline demand as inventories fell last week for the first time since early November An increase in gasoline demand could indicate lower oil prices are supporting demand Oil's gains were capped amid persistent fears over a supply glut that has pulled down prices by 70% since 2014
