Title: U.S. crude stocks fell 2.389 mn barrels vs forecast drop of 1.032 mn
Date: 20161207
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks fell more than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories fell by 2.389 mn barrels after a drop of 884,000 barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 1.032 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks rose by 3.425 mn barrels after a hike of 2.097 mn the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to rise by 1.948 mn barrels. U.S. crude was off 1.71% at $50.06 after data release.
