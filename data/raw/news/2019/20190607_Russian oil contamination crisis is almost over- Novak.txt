Title: Russian oil contamination crisis is almost over: Novak
Date: 20190607
Content:
ST PETERSBURG (Reuters) - Russia's oil contamination crisis is almost over, Energy Minister Alexander Novak said at an economic forum in St Petersburg on Friday.  The country's average daily oil output dropped this month to a three-year low after contaminated crude clogged its main export route. Contaminated Urals crude was discovered in the Druzhba pipeline to Europe in mid-April.
