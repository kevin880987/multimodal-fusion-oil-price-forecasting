Title: Russia to ban imports of fruit from Serbia, Macedonia from Aug. 15
Date: 20180810
Content:
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russia plans to impose a temporary import ban on some fruit from Serbia and Macedonia from Aug. 15 over safety concerns, Russia's food safety regulator said on Friday.  The regulator said it had found the Monilinia fructicola fungus in Serbian peaches and apricots imported since July 13. It said it had also found the same fungus in batches of fruit from Macedonia.
