Title: API reports 5.0 million bbl crude draw, gasoline surges 9.75 mln bbl
Date: 20170118
Content:
Investing.com - The American Petroleum Institute said late Wednesday crude oil inventories dropped 5.0 million barrels in the U.S. at the end of last week, compared to an expected 300,000 barrels build, while gasoline supplies surged 9.75 million barrels. Distillate stocks rose 1.75 million barrels The estimates come ahead of official data from the U.S. Department of Energy on Thursday, delayed by a day because of a public holiday.
