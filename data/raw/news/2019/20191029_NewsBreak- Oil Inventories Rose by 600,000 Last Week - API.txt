Investing.com – U.S. oil inventories rose last week, according to a private survey from the American Petroleum Institute released after the bell Tuesday. The API reported that crude inventories rose by about 600,000 for the week ended Oct. 25. WTI futures were down 0.56% in late trading. Analysts are expecting that the Energy Information Administration’s government data will show a rise of 494,000 in crude inventories tomorrow.
