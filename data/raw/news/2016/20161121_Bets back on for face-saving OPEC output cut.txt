Title: Bets back on for face-saving OPEC output cut
Date: 20161121
Content:
Investing.com - Bets back on for face-saving OPEC-led output cut.OPEC to meet November 30 in Vienna on planned cut to 32.5-33 mn b/d.Deal may revolve around Iran agreeing to freeze output, Russia coming on board.OPEC market credibility at stake after failure to reach accord earlier this year.Saudi verbal intervention to talk up crude prices seen less effective in absence of deal.Watered-down deal would do little to address global glut; plays into U.S. shale producers’ hands.
