Title: U.S. crude stocks up 1.566 mn barrels vs. forecast fall of 453,000
Date: 20170405
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks unexpectedly rose in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories rose by 1.566 mn barrels after a rise of 867,000 barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 453,000 barrels.Gasoline stocks fell by 618,000 barrels after a fall of 3.747 mn barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to fall by 1.422 mn barrels.WTI was up 0.47% at $51.27 after data release.WTI hit a high of $51.88 prior to the release.
