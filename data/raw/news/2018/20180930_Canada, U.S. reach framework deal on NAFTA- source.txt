Title: Canada, U.S. reach framework deal on NAFTA: source
Date: 20180930
Content:
OTTAWA (Reuters) - The United States and Canada have reached a framework deal to update the North American Free Trade Agreement, a Canadian source said late on Sunday.  It involves offering more dairy access to U.S. farmers, as well as Canada agreeing to a side-letter arrangement effectively capping automobile exports to the United States, the source said.
