Title: Russian minister says to discuss output cuts with Russian oil cos on Weds
Date: 20161213
Content:
TEHRAN (Reuters) - Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak said he would discuss with Russian oil companies on Wednesday how they will cut oil production to implement a pact between OPEC and non-OPEC oil-producing nations.  "Each company will determine where it cuts output, on old or new oil fields," he told journalists after talks with his Iranian counterpart in Tehran.
