Title: U.S. energy secretary to Russia: joint work could help market stability
Date: 20180913
Content:
MOSCOW (Reuters) - U.S. Energy Secretary Rick Perry told his Russian counterpart Alexander Novak in Moscow on Thursday that Washington and Moscow could work together as leading energy producers to ensure global market stability, in remarks made in front of reporters. Perry told Novak that both the United States and Russia were interested in the market remaining competitive and that their interests overlapped in the energy sphere.  (This version is refiled to show that remarks were made in front of reporters, not reported to them by Perry)
