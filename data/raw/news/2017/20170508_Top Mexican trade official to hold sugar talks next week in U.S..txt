Title: Top Mexican trade official to hold sugar talks next week in U.S.
Date: 20170508
Content:
MEXICO CITY (Reuters) - Mexican Economy Minister Ildefonso Guajardo will travel to Washington D.C. next week for talks about sugar exports, he told reporters on Monday, in an attempt to break an impasse that threatens to trigger tit-for-tat duties on sweeteners.  Mexico and the United States last week extended a deadline to June 5 to reach an agreement on how much Mexican refined and crude sugar can enter the United States.
