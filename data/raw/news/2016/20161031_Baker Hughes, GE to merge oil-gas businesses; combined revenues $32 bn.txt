Title: Baker Hughes, GE to merge oil/gas businesses; combined revenues $32 bn
Date: 20161031
Content:
Investing.com – GE Monday confirmed oil/gas business merger with Baker Hughes. GE said the combined annual revenues of merged entity amounts to $32 bn. GE to own 62.5%, Baker Hughes shareholders 37.5% of “new” Baker Hughes. GE to contribute $7.4 bn to fund $17.5/share special dividend for Baker Hughes shareholders. Deal seen accretive to GE 2018 EPS by about $.04; $1.6 billion synergies seen by 2020. Lorenzo Simonelli named CEO, Jeff Immelt, chairman, Martin Craighead vice chair of new firm.
