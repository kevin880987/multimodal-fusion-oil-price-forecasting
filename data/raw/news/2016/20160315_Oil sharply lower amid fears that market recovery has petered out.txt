Title: Oil sharply lower amid fears that market recovery has petered out
Date: 20160315
Content:
Investing.com - Oil prices fell more than 2% on Tuesday, extending losses from the previous session on fears that a six-week recovery may have fizzled out. Brent and U.S. crude turned sharply lower after OPEC said that global demand for its crude would be lower than previously thought in 2016. OPEC said supply from rivals is proving more resilient to low prices, increasing the excess supply in the market. Analysts expect a global supply glut to continue to persist with U.S. crude stocks at record highs and hopes for a supply cut fading.
