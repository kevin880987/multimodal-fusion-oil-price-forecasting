Title: Director of Pemex's exploration unit ordered removed from post, banned from holding public office
Date: 20191211
Content:
MEXICO CITY (Reuters) - The director of Mexican state oil firm Pemex's exploration and production unit, Miguel Angel Lozada, has been banned from holding public office for 10 years for alleged fraud, the public ministry said on Wednesday. "The official was notified today of the resolution ... Pemex will have to remove him from post in the company," the ministry said in a statement. Pemex was not immediately available for comment.
