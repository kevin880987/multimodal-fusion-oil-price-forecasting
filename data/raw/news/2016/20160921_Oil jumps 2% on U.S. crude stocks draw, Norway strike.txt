Title: Oil jumps 2% on U.S. crude stocks draw, Norway strike
Date: 20160921
Content:
Investing.com - Oil jumped Wednesday after an unexpected draw in U.S. crude stocks and an oil service worker strike in Norway .U.S. crude was up 96 cents, or 2.18%, at $45.01 at 07:00 ET. Brent crude gained 2.03% to $46.81.American Petroleum Institute data showed a draw of 7.5 million barrels in U.S. crude to 507.2 million.That was the third week in a row that U.S. crude inventories have fallen.Expectations were for an increase of 3.4 million barrels.Official Energy Information Administration figures are due out later Wednesday.The dollar index was slightly lower. A weaker dollar underpins demand for oil.
