Title: Goldman Sachs cuts Q4 WTI forecast to $43
Date: 20160927
Content:
Investing.com – Goldman Sachs (NYSE:GS) Tuesday cut its Q4 WTI forecast to average $43 a barrel from $45-50. GS analysts see Q4 build-up of 400,000 b/d vs. previous view of 300,000 b/d draw.GS demand outlook unchanged at 1.4 mn b/d; higher output by Kazakhstan, Russia and Canada.Output curb deal could support prices in short term as OPEC meets this week in Algiers.But easing output disruptions, speculative positions “leave risks skewed to the downside into year-end.”Sees average price of $52 in 2017 as supply/demand balanced restored.
