Title: Libya's oil revenue rose to $24.4 billion in 2018: NOC
Date: 20190106
Content:
CAIRO (Reuters) - Libya's oil revenue rose to $24.4 billion in 2018, up 78 pct from 2017, Libya's National Oil Corporation (NOC) said in statement on Sunday. NOC also said it recorded an average production of 1.107 million barrels per day in 2018.
