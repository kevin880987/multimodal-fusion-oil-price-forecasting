Title: Oil edges higher
Date: 20160428
Content:
Investing.com - Oil prices were trading close to the prior session's five month highs on Thursday Brent crude futures were trading at $46.97 per barrel at 08:55 GMT, up 4 cents from their last settlement. U.S. Crude futures were up 14 cents at $45.47 a barrel. The dips came after both benchmarks rose on Wednesday to their highest levels for 2016
