Title: Italy tax police search Eni offices in Congo Republic corruption probe: sources
Date: 20180406
Content:
MILAN (Reuters) - Italy's tax police have searched the offices of oil major Eni as part of a probe into alleged corruption in the Republic of Congo, two sources familiar with the matter said on Friday. The sources said the searches had occurred in past days. Eni was not immediately available for a comment. Eni said last year it was being investigated by Milan prosecutors for international corruption relating to agreements signed by its Congo subsidiary with the Congo Republic's Ministry of Hydrocarbons in 2013 to 2015. (This version of the story corrects first paragraph to show the country is Republic of Congo and not Democratic Republic of Congo)
