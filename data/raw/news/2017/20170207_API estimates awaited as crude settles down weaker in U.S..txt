Title: API estimates awaited as crude settles down weaker in U.S.
Date: 20170207
Content:
Investing.com - Crude prices settled down sharply weaker on Tuesday in the U.S. with industry estimates of crude and refined product inventories ahead to set the tone. Global benchmark Brent crude futures dropped 1.20% to to $55.05 a barrel on London's Intercontinental Exchange, while New York Mercantile Exchange, crude oil for delivery in March fell 1.58% to $52.17 a barrel. At 4:30PM ET (21:30GMT), the American Petroleum Institute will release its estimates of U.S. crude and refined product stockpiles. The figures are followed on Wednesday by official data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration. Analysts expect a crude build of 2.38 million barrels at the end of last week.
