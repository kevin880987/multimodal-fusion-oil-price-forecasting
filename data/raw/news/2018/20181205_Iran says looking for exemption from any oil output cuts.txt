Title: Iran says looking for exemption from any oil output cuts
Date: 20181205
Content:
VIENNA (Reuters) - Iran is looking to be exempted from any OPEC oil output cuts because of U.S. sanctions, Iranian Oil Minister Bijan Zanganeh told reporters ahead of Thursday's OPEC meeting.  "We should be excluded from any decision about the level of production in the future till the lifting of the imposed illegal sanctions," Zanganeh told reporters upon arriving in Vienna.
