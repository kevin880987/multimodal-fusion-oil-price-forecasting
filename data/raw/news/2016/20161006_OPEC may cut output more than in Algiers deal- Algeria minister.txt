Title: OPEC may cut output more than in Algiers deal: Algeria minister
Date: 20161006
Content:
Investing.com - OPEC may cut output at November meeting by more than agreed in Algiers.At an informal meeting last month in Algiers OPEC agreed to cut output to 32.5-33.0 mn b/d.That would represent a cut of some 700,000 barrels to address global glut.“If 700,000 barrels are not enough, we will go up,” Algerian Energy Minister Nouredine Bouterfa said.“If we need to cut by 1%, we will cut by 1%," Bouterfa told local Ennahar TV.U.S. crude turned higher to trade up 0.12% at $49.89 at 06:00 ET after an intraday low of $49.34
