Title: Falih: no discussion of deeper oil output cuts at OPEC/non-OPEC meeting
Date: 20170724
Content:
ST PETERSBURG, Russia (Reuters) - Saudi Energy Minister Khalid al-Falih said there would be no discussion of deeper oil output cuts at a meeting of ministers from OPEC and non-OPEC nations on Monday. Ministers from countries including Kuwait, Venezuela, Algeria, Saudi Arabia, Russia and Oman will discuss production caps on Nigeria and Libya, Falih told reporters.
