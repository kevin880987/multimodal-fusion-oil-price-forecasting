Title: Oil Climbs as Big Drop in Inventories Overshadows Record Output
Date: 20190828
Content:
Investing.com - Oil prices climbed Wednesday as a large drawdown in U.S. crude supplies and products overshadowed a surge in U.S. output to record levels. Investing.com - Oil prices climbed Wednesday as a large drawdown in U.S. crude supplies and products overshadowed a surge in U.S. output to record levels.
