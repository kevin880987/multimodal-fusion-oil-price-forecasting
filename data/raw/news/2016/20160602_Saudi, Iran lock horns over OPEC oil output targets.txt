Title: Saudi, Iran lock horns over OPEC oil output targets
Date: 20160602
Content:
Investing.com - Iran snubbed a Saudi proposal for a crude output ceiling before today’s OPEC meeting.Iranian Oil Minister Bijan Zanganeh said a cap is of “no benefit to us.”Iran is looking to boost its market share after sanctions on exports were lifted.The oil cartel is due to issue a statement on the outcome of the meeting at 10:00ET.Brent crude on Thursday hit a high of $50.30 a barrel ahead of the talks in Vienna. U.S. crude futures for July delivery were trading above $49 a barrel.
