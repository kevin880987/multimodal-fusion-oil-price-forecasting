Date: 20190726
Content:

Gold futures for August delivery on the Comex division of the New York Mercantile Exchange, rose $4.95, or 0.4%, to $1,420.01 a troy ounce by 9:03 AM ET (13:03 GMT). The advanced reading for second-quarter GDP saw growth in the U.S. slow from 3.1% in the first three months of the year to 2.1%, better than the expected drop to 1.8%.
