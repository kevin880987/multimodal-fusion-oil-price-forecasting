Title: Saudi oil output policy to remain “responsible”: Energy Minister
Date: 20160831
Content:
Investing.com - Saudi Arabia to maintain “responsible” oil output policy, Energy Minister Khalid al-Falih said.Al-Falih told al-Arabiya TV Wednesday the kingdom doesn`t have a specific output target. “The kingdom's production policy will maintain a large degree of responsibility," he said.Saudi Arabia pumped a record 10.67 million barrels per day in July.OPEC members will hold an informal meeting next month in Algeria.U.S. crude was down 0.35% at $46.19 at 05:45 ET.
