Title: Oil near three-month lows as glut concerns persist
Date: 20160725
Content:
Investing.com – Oil traded near three-month lows on Monday as supply glut concerns continue to weigh.U.S. crude was down 28 cents, or 0.63%, at $43.91 at 06:45 ET, while Brent crude fell 0.61% to $45.81.U.S. crude stockpiles remain at historically high levels, while output remains firm.The number of oil rigs operating in the U.S. rose for the fourth week in a row.Baker Hughes data released Friday showed the U.S. oil rig count up 14 at 371 in the latest week.The dollar index was slightly lower. A weaker dollar underpins demand for oil.
