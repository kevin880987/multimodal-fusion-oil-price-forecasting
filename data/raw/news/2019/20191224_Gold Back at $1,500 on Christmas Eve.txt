Title: Gold Back at $1,500 on Christmas Eve
Date: 20191224
Content:
Investing.com – Gold returned to the key bullish perch of $1,500 an ounce on Christmas Eve as investors continued to seek out the yellow metal as an insurance to a potential Wall Street slide in early 2020. Investing.com – Gold returned to the key bullish perch of $1,500 an ounce on Christmas Eve as investors continued to seek out the yellow metal as an insurance to a potential Wall Street slide in early 2020. Gold futures for February delivery on New York’s COMEX settled up $16.10, or 1.1%, at $1,504.80 per ounce. The last time gold futures traded above $1,500 was on Nov. 5. Stocks on Wall Street have had one of their strongest and most prolonged bull runs this year on optimism over a pending U.S.-China trade deal as well as runaway U.S. jobs growth and other strong data.
