Title: Russia's Novak to discuss global oil deal with Saudi's Falih in November
Date: 20171024
Content:
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak said on Tuesday he plans to discuss a possible extension of the global oil output cut deal and the general situation on world oil markets with Saudi Energy Minister Khalid al-Falih in Riyadh in early November. "This does not mean that we will make any decisions," Novak told reporters.
