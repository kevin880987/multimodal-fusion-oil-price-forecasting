Title: U.S. crude stocks down 1.034 mn barrels vs. forecast fall of 1.470 mn
Date: 20170419
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks fell less than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories fell by 1.034 mn barrels after a fall of 2.166 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 1.470 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks fell by 1.542 mn barrels after a fall of 2.973 mn barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to fall by 1.938 mn barrels.WTI was off 0.06% at $52.38 after data release.
