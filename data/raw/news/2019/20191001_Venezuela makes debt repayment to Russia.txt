Title: Venezuela makes debt repayment to Russia
Date: 20191001
Content:
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Venezuela has made its latest scheduled debt repayment to Russia, Russia's finance ministry spokeswoman said on Tuesday. She did not disclose the amount paid. Close ally Moscow has acted as a lender of last resort for Caracas, with the Russian government and oil giant Rosneft (MM:ROSN) providing at least $17 billion in loans and credit lines since 2006.
