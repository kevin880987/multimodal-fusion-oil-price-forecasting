Title: WTO to rule on China's complaint on U.S. duties on solar cells
Date: 20190815
Content:
GENEVA (Reuters) - The World Trade Organization (WTO) set up a dispute panel requested on Thursday by China to rule on U.S. safeguard duties imposed on imports of solar cells, a Geneva trade official said. The decision, automatic upon its second request at a meeting of the WTO's Dispute Settlement Body (DSB), came as the two countries seek a major trade deal to calm simmering tensions. China charges that the U.S. measure, in the form of a tariff-rate quota on imports of solar cells and an increase in duties on imports of modules, violates WTO rules.
