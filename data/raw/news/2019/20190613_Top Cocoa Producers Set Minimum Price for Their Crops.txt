Title: Top Cocoa Producers Set Minimum Price for Their Crops
Date: 20190613
Content:
(Bloomberg) -- Cocoa buyers and processors meeting in Accra this week agreed to a floor price for beans of $2,600 per ton proposed by top producers Ivory Coast and Ghana, according to the head of Ghana’s cocoa regulator, Joseph Boahen Aidoo.
