Title: Oil rallies as U.S. crude stocks fall more than expected
Date: 20160720
Content:
Investing.com – U.S. crude stocks fell by 2.342 million barrels vs. forecast 2.1 mn draw.U.S. crude futures rallied after sharp losses to trade well above $45 after data.Crude inventories stood at 519.5 mn barrels at the end of the week.Distillate stocks fell by 241,000 vs. forecast rise of 600,000.Gasoline stocks were up 911,000 barrels vs. expected fall of 833,000.
