Title: U.S. crude stocks up 3.295 mn barrels vs. forecast fall of 3.464 mn
Date: 20170607
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks unexpectedly rose in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories rose 3.295 mn barrels after a fall of 6.428 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 3.464 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks rose 3.324 mn barrels after a drop of 2.858 mn barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to rise by 580,000 barrels.WTI slumped 4.38% at $46.08 after data release.
