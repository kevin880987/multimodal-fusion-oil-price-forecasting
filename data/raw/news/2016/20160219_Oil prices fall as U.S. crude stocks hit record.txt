Title: Oil prices fall as U.S. crude stocks hit record
Date: 20160219
Content:
Iinvesting.com - Oil futures fell on Friday as record build in U.S. crude stocks underlined concerns about global oversupply. Brent futures fell 27 cents to $34.01 a barrel. U.S. crude was down 32 cents to $30.45 a barrel. The Energy Information Administration said Thursday U.S. crude stocks rose to record high of 504.1 million barrels last week.
