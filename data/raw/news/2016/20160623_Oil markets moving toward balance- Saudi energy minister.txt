Title: Oil markets moving toward balance: Saudi energy minister
Date: 20160623
Content:
Investing.com - Oil market supply and demand are almost in balance, Saudi energy minister said."Supply and demand are almost equal," Khalid al-Falih said Thursday."The markets have started to improve in response to this," al-Falih added.Brent moved toward $51 on Thursday, while U.S. tested the $50 mark.Falih is visiting the U.S. with Saudi Deputy Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman.The visit aimed at repairing frayed relations with Washington.
