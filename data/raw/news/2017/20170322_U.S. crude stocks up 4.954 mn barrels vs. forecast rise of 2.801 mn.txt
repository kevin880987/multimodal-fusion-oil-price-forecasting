Title: U.S. crude stocks up 4.954 mn barrels vs. forecast rise of 2.801 mn
Date: 20170322
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks rose more than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories rose by 4.954 mn barrels after a fall of 237,000 barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to rise by 2.801 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks fell by 2.811 mn barrels after a fall of 3.055 mn barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to fall by 2.008 mn barrels.U.S. crude was off 1.80% at $47.37 after data release.
