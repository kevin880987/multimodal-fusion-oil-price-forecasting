Title: Russia's Novak plans to meet Saudi's Falih at Davos: report
Date: 20190117
Content:
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak plans to meet Saudi Energy Minister Khalid al-Falih at the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland next week, Interfax news agency cited the Russian minister as saying on Thursday.  The WEF's annual gathering of political and business leaders is due to take place from Jan. 23 to 26 in the Swiss ski resort.
