Title: OPEC sees bigger oil surplus in 2017 if cuts not met
Date: 20161214
Content:
Investing.com - OPEC Wednesday signaled a bigger oil supply surplus next year if producers do not adhere to agreed cuts.OPEC pumped 33.87 mn b/d last month, according to figures OPEC collects from secondary sources.OPEC has agreed to cut output by 1.2 mn b/d from 2017. Non-OPEC producers to chip in with 558,000 b/d cut.The IEA sees supply deficit of 600,000 b/d in H1 2017 if cuts are implemented.U.S. crude was down 1.45% at $52.21 at 07:45 ET.
