Title: OPEC experts meet in Vienna on planned output cut
Date: 20161028
Content:
Investing.com – OPEC experts Friday gather in Vienna for two-day meeting on planned output cut.OPEC members reached a tentative accord last month to cut output to 32.5-33 mn barrels a day.Chances of firm accord in the air as Iran and Iraq insist on not curbing output.Non-OPEC members, including Russia, due to join meeting on Saturday.Technical committee expected to discuss quotas for individual members.OPEC to hold formal meeting in Vienna on November 30.
