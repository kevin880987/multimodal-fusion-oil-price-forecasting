Title: NewsBreak: Crude Inventories Jump 10.5M, API Data Shows
Date: 20191016
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stockpiles rose sharply last week, according to an estimate from the American Petroleum Institute released Wednesday. The API said crude inventories rose by 10.5 million for the week ended Oct. 11. Analysts expect that the Energy Information Administration will report a build of 2.88 million barrels when it reports numbers tomorrow morning. The EIA petroelum data are due at 10:30 AM ET (14:30 GMT). WTI futures were up 0.4% at $53.02 following the release, after settling up 1% today in regular trading.
