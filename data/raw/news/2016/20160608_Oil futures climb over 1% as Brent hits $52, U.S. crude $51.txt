Title: Oil futures climb over 1% as Brent hits $52, U.S. crude $51
Date: 20160608
Content:
Oil futures surged on Wednesday as U.S. crude stockpiles fell and demand from China rose strongly. Brent put on 70 cents, or 1.36%, to $52.14 as U.S. crude climbed 65 cents, or 1.29%, to $51.01 at 06:45 ET. American Petroleum Institute estimates released Tuesday showed crude inventories fell by 3.56 million barrels in the latest week, the third fall in a row. The U.S. Energy Information Administration is to release its weekly inventories report at 10:30 ET. A 38.7% jump in Chinese crude imports in May and production outages in Nigeria helped assuage supply glut concerns.
