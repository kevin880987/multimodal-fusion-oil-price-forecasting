Title: U.S. crude stocks down 4.432 mn barrels vs. forecast fall of 2.419 mn
Date: 20170524
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks fell more than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories fell by 4.432 mn barrels after a fall of 1.753 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 2.419 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks fell by 787,000 barrels after a drop of 413,000 barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to fall by 1.194 mn barrels.WTI was up 0.14% at $51.54 after data release.
