Title: U.S. motor travel increased by 2.9 percent in September: DOT
Date: 20161205
Content:
NEW YORK (Reuters) - U.S. motorists logged 2.9 percent more miles in September versus last year, continuing a record-setting pace, according to new data released Monday by the U.S. Department of Transportation. Through September, motorists logged 2.414 billion miles on U.S. roads and highways, the most ever during the first nine months of the year, federal data shows.
