Title: ConocoPhillips to raise $5 billion - $8 billion from asset sales
Date: 20161110
Content:
(Reuters) - ConocoPhillips (N:COP), the largest U.S. independent oil producer, said it was looking to raise $5 billion to $8 billion from the sale of assets, particularly natural gas-producing operations in North America.  The company, which is attempting to shore up its balance sheet after a steep slide in oil prices that began in mid 2014, also said it would buy back $3 billion of its stock.
