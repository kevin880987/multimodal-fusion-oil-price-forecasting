Title: Russian energy minister says hopes for oil deal in Doha
Date: 20160413
Content:
YEREVAN (Reuters) - Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak said on Wednesday that he hoped the deal to freeze oil production at the Doha meeting on April 17 would be reached. "We are optimistic, we will go there for an agreement," he told reporters during his visit to Yerevan.  He also said that 17 countries plan to take part in the meeting in Doha.
