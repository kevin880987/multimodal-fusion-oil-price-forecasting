Title: U.S. crude stocks down 5.247 mn barrels vs. forecast fall of 1.786 mn
Date: 20170510
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks fell more than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories fell by 5.247 mn barrels after a fall of 930,000 barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 1.786 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks fell by 150,000 barrels after a rise of 191,000 barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to fall by 538,000 barrels.U.S. crude was up 2.20% at $46.89 after data release.
