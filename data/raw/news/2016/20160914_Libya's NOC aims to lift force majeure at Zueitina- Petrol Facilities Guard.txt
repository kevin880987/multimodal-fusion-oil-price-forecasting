Title: Libya's NOC aims to lift force majeure at Zueitina: Petrol Facilities Guard
Date: 20160914
Content:
BENGHAZI, Libya (Reuters) - The chairman of Libya's National Oil Corporation (NOC) visited Zueitina port on Wednesday and said he would work to lift force majeure there, according to the head of a guard force now in control of the terminal.  The visit by NOC Chairman Mustafa Sanalla comes days after forces loyal to eastern commander Khalifa Haftar seized Zueitina and three other oil ports from a rival force allied to the U.N.-backed government in Tripoli.
