Title: OPEC agrees 1.2 mn b/d output cut: reports
Date: 20161130
Content:
Investing.com - OPEC Wednesday agreed to a 1.2 mn b/d cut in output to 32.5 mn: Bloomberg.Bloomberg quoted a source as saying that the deal is not yet public.Reuters also quoted an OPEC source as saying accord reached to cut to 32.5 mn b/d.If confirmed this would be the first cut by OPEC since 2008.OPEC is due to hold a news conference at 10:00 ET.U.S. crude was up 6.92% at $48.36 at 09:30 ET
