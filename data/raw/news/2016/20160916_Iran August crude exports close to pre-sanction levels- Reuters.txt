Title: Iran August crude exports close to pre-sanction levels: Reuters
Date: 20160916
Content:
Investing.com - Iran's August crude exports jumped 15% from July to over 2 mn bpd, sources said Friday.That was close to pre-sanction levels, adding to a supply glut, Reuters reported.Iran has doubled crude exports this year as it seeks to regain market share.Major producers are due to meet later this month for talks on the oil glut.Earlier this year Iran snubbed a proposed freeze on output.
