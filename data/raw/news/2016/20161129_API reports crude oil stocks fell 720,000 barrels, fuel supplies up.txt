Title: API reports crude oil stocks fell 720,000 barrels, fuel supplies up
Date: 20161129
Content:
Investing.com - The American Petroleum Institute late Tuesday said crude oil inventories fell 720,000 barrels last week, 120,000 barrels more than expected and which followed a draw of 1.28 million barrels the previous week. Gasoline inventories jumped 3.4 million barrels last week, and distillates rose 2.2 million barrels, the largest build in more than over two months. Cushing recorded a gain of 2.4 million barrels.
