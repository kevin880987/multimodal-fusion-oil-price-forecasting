Title: EU measures against U.S. tariffs will not hit Norway: Juncker
Date: 20180605
Content:
BRUSSELS (Reuters) - The European Union would make sure any measures aimed and counteracting U.S. trade tariffs on aluminum and steel would not hit Norway, EU Commission President Juncker said on Tuesday.  "As we consider safeguard measures, I have assured the Prime Minister that we are working on solution to soften any possible knock-on effect this would have on Norway. In fact there will be no impact at all on Norway," Juncker told a news conference after a meeting with Norway Prime Minister Erna Solberg.
