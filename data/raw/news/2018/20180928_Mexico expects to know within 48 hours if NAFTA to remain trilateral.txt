Title: Mexico expects to know within 48 hours if NAFTA to remain trilateral
Date: 20180928
Content:
MEXICO CITY (Reuters) - Mexican Economy Minister Ildefonso Guajardo said on Friday that the United States and Canada are making a serious attempt to reach a deal on the North American Free Trade Agreement, saying that within 48 hours it would become clear if the deal would remain trilateral. "In the next 48 hours we will know if we're going to go with a trilateral text (that includes Canada) or if we're going to be forced to publish a text of the bilateral (U.S.-Mexico) deal," Guajardo told lawmakers.
