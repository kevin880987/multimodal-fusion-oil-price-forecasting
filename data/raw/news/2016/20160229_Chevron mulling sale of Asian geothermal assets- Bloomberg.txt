Title: Chevron mulling sale of Asian geothermal assets: Bloomberg
Date: 20160229
Content:
(Reuters) -  Chevron  Corp (N:CVX), the No. 2 U.S. oil producer, is considering a sale of its geothermal assets in Asia, Bloomberg reported citing people familiar with the matter.  The company is planning to sell the assets to offset the prolonged slump in oil prices, Bloomberg reported on Monday. (bloom.bg/1TMoHgT)
