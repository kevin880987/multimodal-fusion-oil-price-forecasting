Title: Russia's Novak says all oil firms to cut output under OPEC deal
Date: 20161216
Content:
TOKYO (Reuters) - All Russian oil companies, including champion producer Rosneft (MM:ROSN), have agreed to cut crude output under Moscow's agreements with OPEC, Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak said on Friday.  Asked if Rosneft would cut production alongside other producers, Novak told reporters: "All companies agree to cut output."
