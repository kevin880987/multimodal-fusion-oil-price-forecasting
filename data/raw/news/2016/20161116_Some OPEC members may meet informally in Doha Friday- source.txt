Title: Some OPEC members may meet informally in Doha Friday: source
Date: 20161116
Content:
Investing.com – Some OPEC members may hold informal meeting in Doha Friday, Reuters quoted an Algerian source as saying. OPEC is trying to broker an accord to cut output to 32.5-33 mn b/d after record production in October of 33.64 mn.Key to any deal rests on reaching a consensus on quotas for individual members.OPEC is due to hold a formal meeting in Vienna on November 30.Doha is hosting the Gas Exporting Countries Forum this week.U.S. crude was down 0.85% at $45.42 at 05:30 ET after surging overnight.
