Title: U.S. crude stocks up 6.466 mn barrels vs. forecast rise of 3.289 mn
Date: 20170201
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks rose more than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories rose by 6.466 mn barrels after a rise of 2.840 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to rise by 3.289 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks rose by 3.866 mn barrels after a hike of 6.796 mn the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to rise by 982,000 barrels.U.S. crude was up 0.64% at $53.15 after data release.
