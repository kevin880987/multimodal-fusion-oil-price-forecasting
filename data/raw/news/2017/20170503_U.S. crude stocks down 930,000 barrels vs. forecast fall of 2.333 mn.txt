Title: U.S. crude stocks down 930,000 barrels vs. forecast fall of 2.333 mn
Date: 20170503
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks fell less than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories fell by 930,000 barrels after a fall of 3.641 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 2.333 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks rose by 191,000 barrels after a rise of 3.369 mn barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to rise by 1.322 mn barrels.U.S. crude gave up earlier gains and was off 0.36% at $47.49 after data release.
