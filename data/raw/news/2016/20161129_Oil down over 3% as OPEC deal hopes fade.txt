Title: Oil down over 3% as OPEC deal hopes fade
Date: 20161129
Content:
Investing.com - Oil Tuesday extended losses to trade down over 3% as hopes of an OPEC deal to cut output faded.U.S. crude was down 3.42% at $45.47 at 10:30 ET, while Brent crude gave up 3.35% to $47.56.Indonesia's energy minister said he was "not optimistic" of reaching a deal before OPEC's formal meeting tomorrow.OPEC agreed in September to negotiate a cut to 32.5-33 mn b/d.There are doubts that Iraq and Iran will come on board a deal.
