Title: Saudi minister hopes global slowdown won’t impact oil
Date: 20160712
Content:
Investing.com – Saudi’s energy minister hopes slower global growth won’t impact crude oil.“At the moment we see healthy demand for oil," Khalid al-Falih told Handelsblatt.But he noted “economic headwinds” in some important markets.“We hope this does not trigger a slowdown in global demand," he added.Al-Falih said it would take a “long time” to reduce crude inventory overhang.
