Title: Trump to sign trade agreements with Japan on Monday: White House
Date: 20191007
Content:
WASHINGTON (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump will sign two trade agreements with Japan later on Monday, the White House said in a statement. Trump is scheduled to sign the pacts -- U.S.-Japan Trade Agreement and U.S.-Japan Digital Trade Agreement -- at 3:30 p.m. (1930 GMT), it said. Trump and Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe held a signing ceremony on the sidelines of the United Nations General Assembly last month after reaching a limited deal.
