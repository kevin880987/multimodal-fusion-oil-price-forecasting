Title: Oil producer Halcon Resources files for bankruptcy protection
Date: 20160727
Content:
(Reuters) -  Halcon  Resources Corp (N:HK), which produces oil in Texas and North Dakota, filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection on Wednesday. The company listed assets and liabilities in the range of $1 billion to $10 billion, according to a filing with the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the District of Delaware. In May, Halcon stated it intends to file for a prepackaged bankruptcy that would wipe out $1.8 billion in debt and help it survive the drop in crude prices.
