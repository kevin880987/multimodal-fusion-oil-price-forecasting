Title: Oil recovers from overnight losses as stockpile glut persists
Date: 20160714
Content:
Investing.com – Oil prices recovered from sharp overnight losses as a glut in crude stocks persisted.U.S. crude was up 53 cents, or 1.18%, at $45.28 at 07:00 ET, while Brent crude rose 1.19% to $46.81.The International Energy Agency (IEA) Wednesday said the crude stock overhang posed a serious threat to oil price stability.Crude futures fell by 4% overnight after the release of the IEA’s latest monthly report.Energy Information Administration figures Wednesday showed U.S. crude inventories fell by 2.5 million barrels in the latest reporting week.Forecasts were for a draw of close to 3 million barrels. Stocks remain at historical highs at 521.8 million barrels.Gasoline inventories rose by 1.2 million barrels despite the U.S. summer driving season.The dollar index was down slightly. A weaker dollar underpins demand for oil.
