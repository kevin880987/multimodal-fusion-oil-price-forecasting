Title: Russia to discuss output cut details with national oil firms: minister
Date: 20161213
Content:
Investing. com - Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak said he will discuss implementation of output cuts with local oil firms.Novak said the talks are planned to take place on Wednesday."Each company will determine where it cuts output, on old or new oil fields," he told journalists.Russia has pledged to cut by some 300,000 b/d as part of reduction by non-OPEC producers of 558,000 b/d.That came on top of an accord by OPEC to cut by 1.2 million barrels a day
