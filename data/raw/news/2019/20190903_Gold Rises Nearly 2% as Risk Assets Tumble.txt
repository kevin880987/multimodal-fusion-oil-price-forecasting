Title: Gold Rises Nearly 2% as Risk Assets Tumble
Date: 20190903
Content:
By Barani Krishnan Investing.com - Already up 20% this year, the rally in gold is still going, fed by fears over the trade war and the potential for the world to plunge into a recession. Investing.com - Already up 20% this year, the rally in gold is still going, fed by fears over the trade war and the potential for the world to plunge into a recession.
