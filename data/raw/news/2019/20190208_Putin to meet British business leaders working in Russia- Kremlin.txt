Title: Putin to meet British business leaders working in Russia: Kremlin
Date: 20190208
Content:
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian President Vladimir Putin has agreed to a proposal from  BP  (LON:BP) CEO Robert Dudley to hold a meeting with heads of British companies working in Russia, Kremlin spokesman Dmitry Peskov told reporters on Friday. Peskov was commenting on Putin's meeting with Dudley the previous day. He also said BP has and will always have the support of Russian authorities.
