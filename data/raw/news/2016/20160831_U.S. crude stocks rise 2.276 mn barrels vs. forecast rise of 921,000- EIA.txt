Title: U.S. crude stocks rise 2.276 mn barrels vs. forecast rise of 921,000: EIA
Date: 20160831
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks rose more than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude stocks rose by 2.276 mn barrels after a rise of 2.501 mn the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to rise by 921,000 barrels.Gasoline stocks barrels fell 691,000 barrels after a rise of 36,000 the previous week.Gasoline inventories were forecast to fall by 1.157 mn barrels.U.S. crude was down 1.98% at $45.43 after the data release.
