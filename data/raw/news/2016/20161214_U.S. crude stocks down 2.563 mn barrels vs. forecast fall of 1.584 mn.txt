Title: U.S. crude stocks down 2.563 mn barrels vs. forecast fall of 1.584 mn
Date: 20161214
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks fell more than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories fell by 2.563 mn barrels after a drop of 2.389 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to fall by 1.584 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks rose by 497,000 barrels after a hike of 3.425 mn the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to rise by 2.540 mn barrels.U.S. crude pared losses after the data. It was off 0.83% at $52.54 at 10:45 ET.
