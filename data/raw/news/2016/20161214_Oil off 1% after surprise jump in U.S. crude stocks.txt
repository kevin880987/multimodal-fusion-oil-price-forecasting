Title: Oil off 1% after surprise jump in U.S. crude stocks
Date: 20161214
Content:
Investing.com - Oil was lower Wednesday after an unexpected jump in U.S. crude stocks.U.S. crude fell 77 cents, or 1.45%, to $52.21 at 07:45 ET. Brent crude shed 1.36% to $54.96.American Petroleum Institute industry data showed a jump of 4.7 million barrels in U.S. crude inventories in the latest week.Official Energy Information Administration inventories figures due out later Wednesday are forecast to show a fall of about 1.5 million barrels.Investors are looking to see how producers comply with agreed cuts in output of 1.8 million barrels a day.The International Energy Agency Tuesday predicted if the cuts are fully implemented there could be a supply deficit of 600,000 barrels a day in the first half of next year.The dollar index fell below the 101 mark ahead of the FOMC meeting. A weaker dollar underpins demand for oil.
