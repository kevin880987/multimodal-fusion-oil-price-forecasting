Title: U.S. crude regains $50 mark for first time since late June
Date: 20161006
Content:
Investing.com – U.S. crude Thursday moved above $50 for the first time since late June.U.S. crude was up 0.78% at $50.22 at 09:00 ET, while Brent crude was up 0.79% at $52.27.The market is being buoyed by a big drawdown in U.S. crude stocks in the past week.Support is also coming from hopes OPEC will flesh out an agreement to cut output at its November formal meeting.
