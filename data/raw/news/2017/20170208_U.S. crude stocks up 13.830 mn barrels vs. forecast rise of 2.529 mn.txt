Title: U.S. crude stocks up 13.830 mn barrels vs. forecast rise of 2.529 mn
Date: 20170208
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks rose more than expected in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude inventories rose by 13.830 mn barrels after a rise of 6.466 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to rise by 2.529 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks fell by 869,000 barrels after a hike of 3.866 mn the previous week.Gasoline inventories were expected to rise by 1.071 mn barrels.U.S. crude was off 0.71% at $51.80 just after the data release.
