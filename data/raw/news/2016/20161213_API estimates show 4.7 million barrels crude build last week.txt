Title: API estimates show 4.7 million barrels crude build last week
Date: 20161213
Content:
Investing.com - The American Petroleum Institute said late Tuesday that U.S. crude oil inventories soared 4.7 million barrels last week, well above an expected 1.5 million barrel drop. Gasoline inventories rose 3.9 million barrels and distillates gained 200,000 barrels, while Cushing showed a 600,000 barrel increase.
