Title: Mexican ministers head to Washington amid NAFTA talks: Mexico government
Date: 20180419
Content:
MEXICO CITY (Reuters) - Mexico's foreign minister and economy minister will meet U.S. officials in Washington on Thursday and Friday, the Mexican government said, as ministers from Mexico, the United States and Canada gather there to push for a renegotiated NAFTA trade deal.  Foreign Minister Luis Videgaray will meet U.S. President Donald Trump's son-in-law and advisor, Jared Kushner, while Economy Minister Ildefonso Guajardo will meet U.S. Trade Representative Robert Lighthizer, the foreign ministry said in a statement.
