Title: Oil turns mixed after earlier losses
Date: 20160923
Content:
Investing.com – Oil Friday turned mixed after earlier losses after a reported Saudi offer to cut output.U.S. crude was down 5 cents, or 0.11%, at $46.27 at 07:00 ET, while Brent crude gained 0.59% to $47.93 Reuters quoted sources as saying Saudi Arabia will cut output if Iran agrees to freeze its productionMajor producers are due to hold informal talks in Algeria next week.Oil fell earlier in the session after strong gains on a surprise draw in U.S. crude stocks and a weaker dollarBaker Hughes U.S. rig count data are due out later in the session. The dollar index was slightly higher. A stronger dollar dampens demand for oil.
