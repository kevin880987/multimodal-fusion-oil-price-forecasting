Title: OPEC cuts 2016 global growth forecast; upbeat on oil for 2017
Date: 20160712
Content:
Investing.com- OPEC cut its 2016 global growth forecast but upbeat on Crude Oil for 2017. OPEC cut its estimate for global growth to 3.0% from 3.1% in wake of Brexit. “Economic uncertainty has increased,” after Brexit OPEC said in its June report. Cartel sees world oil demand rising by 1.15 million barrels per day in 2017. Expects excess oil inventories to be whittled down.
