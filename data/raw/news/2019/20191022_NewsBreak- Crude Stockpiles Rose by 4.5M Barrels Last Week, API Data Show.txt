Title: NewsBreak: Crude Stockpiles Rose by 4.5M Barrels Last Week, API Data Show
Date: 20191022
Content:

Investing.com – U.S. crude stockpiles rose 4.5 million last week, a third-straight weekly build, according to an estimate from the American Petroleum Institute released Tuesday. The API said crude inventories rose by 4.5 million for the week ended Oct. 18. WTI futures were up 1.3% at $54.20 following the release, after settling up 1.6% today in regular trading. Analysts expect that the Energy Information Administration will report a build of 2.23 million barrels when it reports numbers tomorrow morning. The EIA petroelum data are due at 10:30 AM ET (14:30 GMT).
