Title: U.S. crude stocks fall 2.976 mn barrels vs. forecast rise of 2.560 mn
Date: 20161005
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks unexpectedly fell in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude stocks fell by 2.976 mn barrels after a fall of 1.882 mn barrels the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to rise by 2.560 mn barrels.Gasoline stocks rose by 222,000 barrels after a gain of 2.072 mn barrels the previous week.Gasoline inventories were forecast to rise by 702,000 barrels.U.S. crude was up $0.97, or 1.99%, at $49.66 after data release.
