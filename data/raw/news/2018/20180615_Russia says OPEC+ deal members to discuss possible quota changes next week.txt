Title: Russia says OPEC+ deal members to discuss possible quota changes next week
Date: 20180615
Content:
MOSCOW (Reuters) - OPEC and non-OPEC nations that are part of global oil production curbs plan to discuss possible changes in the oil production cut quotas at a meeting in Vienna on June 23, the Russian Energy Ministry said on Friday.  The ministry also said that participants in the OPEC+ deal plan to discuss framework principles for further cooperation between OPEC and non-OPEC countries.
