Title: Saudi Aramco signs $10.4 billion worth of MOUs with companies
Date: 20171213
Content:
DAMMAM, Saudi Arabia (Reuters) - Saudi Aramco [IPO-ARMO.SE] signed on Wednesday agreements with foreign and local companies worth at least 39 billion riyals (8 billion pounds).  The bulk of the memorandum of understanding agreements were part of a drive to promote local manufacturing, the company said. One agreement was also signed with drilling and oil service company Schlumberger (SA:SLBG34) worth 6 billion riyals.
