Title: U.S. crude stocks down 2.508 mn barrels vs. forecast rise of 522,000: EIA
Date: 20160817
Content:
Investing.com - U.S. crude stocks unexpectedly fell in the latest week, official data showed Wednesday.The EIA said crude stocks fell by 2.508 mn barrels vs. rise of 1.055 mn the previous week.Crude inventories were forecast to rise by 522,000 barrels.Gasoline stocks fell 2.724 mn barrels after a drop of 2.807 mn the previous week.Gasoline inventories were forecast to fall by 1.638 mn barrels.
