Title: Brazil court approves contract draft for transfer of rights oil deal
Date: 20191023
Content:
SAO PAULO (Reuters) - Brazil's federal audits court on Wednesday approved a draft contract for a massive transfer of rights oil deal between state-run oil firm Petroleo Brasileiro SA (SA:PETR4), better known as Petrobras, and the government. The highly anticipated auction, which takes place next month, could take in $26 billion from the combined signing bonuses bidders who win exploration and production rights will be obliged to pay the government.
