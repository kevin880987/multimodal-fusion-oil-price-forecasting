Title: Trump says there is a 'really good chance' of U.S.-China trade deal
Date: 20191009
Content:
WASHINGTON (Reuters) - President Donald Trump said on Wednesday there was a very good chance that the United States and China will reach a trade agreement. Speaking to reporters a day before high-level trade talks resume in Washington, Trump said: "If we can make a deal, we're going to make a deal, there's a really good chance." "In my opinion China wants to make a deal more than I do," he said.
