Title: Oil off over 1% as OPEC deal doubts grow
Date: 20161129
Content:
Investing.com - Oil fell over 1% Tuesday as doubts about OPEC's ability to deliver a planned cut in output grew.U.S. crude was down 82 cents, or 1.74%, at $46.26 a barrel at 06:45 ET, while Brent crude shed 1.61% to $48.42.OPEC experts ended talks Monday without any agreement on quotas for individual members in any curbs on production.OPEC reached a tentative accord in Algiers in September to cut output to 32.5-33 million barrels a day. The cartel is due to hold a formal meeting in Vienna on Wednesday.The dollar Index steadied Tuesday above the 101 level. A stronger dollar undermines demand for oil.The American Petroleum Institute (API) is due to release industry stockpile figures later in the session.
