Title: OPEC Sec-Gen sees growing consensus on duration of oil cut extension
Date: 20170522
Content:
VIENNA (Reuters) - OPEC Secretary-General Mohammad Barkindo said on Monday he saw a growing consensus among the group's members as well as non-OPEC producers on the duration of oil cuts extension.  OPEC will meet on Thursday to decide on its production policies after June. OPEC leader Saudi Arabia and non-OPEC Russia have said they see a need to extend existing output cuts for another nine months to March 2018.
