Title: Saudi Aramco to sign 15 deals worth more than $30 billion: state TV
Date: 20181023
Content:
DUBAI (Reuters) - Oil giant Saudi Aramco will sign 15 deals worth more than $30 billion, said Saudi state-owned TV Ekhbariya. Saudi Arabia plans to sign deals worth more than $50 billion in the oil, gas, industries and infrastructure sectors at an investment conference in Riyadh on Tuesday, a source familiar with the matter told Reuters earlier.
