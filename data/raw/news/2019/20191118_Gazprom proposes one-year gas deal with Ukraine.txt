Title: Gazprom proposes one-year gas deal with Ukraine
Date: 20191118
Content:
MOSCOW (Reuters) - Gazprom (MM:GAZP) has sent a formal letter to Ukraine proposing either to extend a current gas deal or sign a new one-year agreement, the Russian gas exporter said on Monday . It also said that it had offered to drop legal claims and counter-claims by both Ukraine and Russia as a pre-condition for the deal. The current deal on Russian gas supplies to Ukraine and its transit via the country to Europe expires on Dec. 31.
