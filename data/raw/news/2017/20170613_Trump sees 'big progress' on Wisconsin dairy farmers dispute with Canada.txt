Title: Trump sees 'big progress' on Wisconsin dairy farmers dispute with Canada
Date: 20170613
Content:
PEWAUKEE, Wis. (Reuters) - President Donald Trump said on Tuesday he was making progress on behalf of Wisconsin dairy farmers in a dispute with Canada over its trade practices. "We are making big progress on opening things up," he said on a visit to the Waukesha County Technical College in Wisconsin, a major cheese-producing state.
