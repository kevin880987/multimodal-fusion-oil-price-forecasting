Date: 20190805
Content:

Spot gold gained $24.57, or 1.7%, to $1,465.56 by 10:04 AM ET (14:04 GMT). That was close to the intraday high of $1,466.33, its most expensive level since May 9, 2013.
Risk aversion hit other markets, with money tumbling out of stocks and into bonds, as well as gold. The S&P 500, Dow and Nasdaq Composite all sank more than 2%.
