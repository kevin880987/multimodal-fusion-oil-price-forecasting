Title: OPEC shouldn’t stem supply too much: Saudi minister
Date: 20161010
Content:
Investing.com – OPEC should not stem oil supply too much, Saudi Energy Minister said.Khalid al-Falih said Monday he was optimistic about OPEC output deal next month.OPEC agreed to cut output to 32.5-33 mn b/d in informal meeting in Algiers last month.Details of implementation of deal to be thrashed out at OPEC formal meeting in Vienna on November 30.Non-OPEC producers may also need to cut for effective deal to address global supply.U.S. crude was down 27 cents, or 0.54% at $49.54 at 04:45 ET.
